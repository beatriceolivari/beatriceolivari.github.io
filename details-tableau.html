Dashboard of Airbnb in Madrid

In my job I use PowerBI as a tool to create dashboards, but I was curious to learn Tableau too.

Therefore I started playing with it and I found this data about Madrid Airbnb bookings.

As I spent one year living in the city I was curious to analyse the data and see if the outcomes would be as I expected.
